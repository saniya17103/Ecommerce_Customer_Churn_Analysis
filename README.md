# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis & Prediction

> **An end-to-end Customer Analytics project focused on identifying churn patterns, understanding customer behavior, and generating actionable business insights to improve customer retention and business growth.**

---

## 🌟 Project Overview

Customer retention is one of the most important factors influencing long-term business success in the e-commerce industry. Acquiring new customers is often more expensive than retaining existing ones, making churn analysis a critical business function.

This project analyzes customer purchasing behavior using the **Brazilian E-Commerce (Olist) Dataset** and leverages **SQL**, **Python**, and **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** techniques to identify customers at risk of churn and uncover the factors driving customer attrition.

The project transforms raw transactional data into meaningful customer-level insights, enabling data-driven decision-making and supporting effective customer retention strategies.

## 🔍 Churn Definition

> A customer is classified as **Churned** if no purchase activity is recorded for more than **180 days**.

| Churn Flag | Status |
|------------|---------|
| 🔴 1 | Churned |
| 🟢 0 | Active |

## 📈 Key Insights

### ⭐ Customer Satisfaction Drives Retention
Customers with lower review ratings showed a significantly higher tendency to churn.

### 🚚 Delivery Performance Impacts Loyalty
Late deliveries and longer waiting times negatively influenced customer retention.

### 💰 Freight Costs Affect Customer Behavior
Higher shipping costs were associated with increased churn probability.

### 🔁 Repeat Customers Generate Higher Revenue
Customers with multiple purchases contributed substantially more value than one-time buyers.
